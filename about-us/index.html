Rob Fox

Robert J. Fox Jr. was born and raised in Eustis. He has never left, and that is not an accident. This is his community, these are his neighbors, and that shapes how he runs the shop. When you bring your car to RJ Fox Automotive, you are dealing with someone who has a stake in this town and a reputation to protect in it.

Rob started working on cars in the 1980s, taught himself the trade, and has spent more than four decades at it. He built his skills the way most good mechanics do, by working on real cars with real problems, learning what works and what does not, and developing the kind of diagnostic instinct that only comes from years of hands-on experience. There is no shortcut to that.

Why He Started the Shop

Rob started RJ Fox Automotive to do honest work. That sounds simple, but it is harder to find than it should be in the auto repair industry. Too many shops recommend work that is not needed, quote one price and charge another, or send the hard jobs out to a specialty rebuilder and mark up the cost. Rob built this shop around doing the opposite of all of that.

You get a diagnosis before you get a recommendation. You get a written estimate before anyone touches your car. If something changes during the repair, you get a call before the work continues. The bill matches what was discussed. That is how it has worked here since the beginning.

Transmission Work

Transmission repair is what RJ Fox Automotive is known for throughout Lake County. While most general repair shops send transmission jobs to an outside rebuilder and mark up the cost, Rob does that work in-house. Automatic, manual, CVT, clutch systems, the full range. That means better quality control, direct communication with the person actually doing the work, and pricing that does not include a middleman.

If you have been quoted a transmission repair somewhere else and want a second opinion, or if you have gotten conflicting information about what your vehicle actually needs, bring it in. Rob will tell you what he finds and what it costs to fix, and he will be straight with you if the repair does not make sense for the vehicle.

Serving Eustis and Lake County

RJ Fox Automotive is at 801 David Walker Dr in Eustis, a short drive from Mount Dora, Tavares, Leesburg, and Clermont. Rob and his team work on all makes and models, domestic and import, gas and diesel. They have been doing it here for over 40 years and they are not going anywhere.
