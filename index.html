About Rob Fox

Born Here.
Still Here.

Robert J. Fox Jr. was born and raised in Eustis. He started working on cars in the 1980s, built his skills the way most good mechanics do (on real cars with real problems) and has been doing it here for over four decades. This is his community. That shapes how he runs the shop.

Most shops in Lake County send transmission jobs to an outside rebuilder and mark up the cost. We do it in-house. That means better quality control, direct communication with the person doing the work, and pricing that does not include a middleman. If you have been getting different answers from different shops, bring it to us. We will give you an accurate diagnosis in writing before anyone touches your car.
