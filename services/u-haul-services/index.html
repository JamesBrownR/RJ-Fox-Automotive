  • In-town rentals for local moves within Lake County

Tips for a Smoother Move

Reserve early, especially on weekends and at the end of the month when demand is highest. If you are not sure what truck size you need, call us and describe your load — we can help you pick the right one so you are not making two trips or paying for more truck than you need.

Return the vehicle with a full tank to avoid fuel charges. Check the truck for existing damage before you drive off and make sure it is noted on your contract. If anything comes up during your rental, call us directly and we will help sort it out.
